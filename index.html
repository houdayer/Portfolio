I am a user experience and interface designer based in Berlin.
My aim is to have an impact on product usability and customer satisfaction. I work on the full design process, however I have a special affinity for micro-interactions in UI.
I worked for 8 months, in Paris and Berlin, as a UX / UI Design Teacher.
I gave workshops to junior designers, helped in more than 500 projects in the span of three       9-week bootcamps.
I gained experience in explaining the design concepts, having an eye for detail and facilitating group work.
