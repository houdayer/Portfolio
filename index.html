From research to prototyping and user testing I like to work on the full design process, I am both UX focused and UI detail-oriented. I go the « extra-mile », producing high-fidelity prototypes with animations to have a result as close as possible to the validated design, thus working hand-in-hand with developers.

I used to be a UX/UI Design Teacher Assistant. My role was to transmit UX methodologies to junior designers, give UI principles workshops on Sketch, Figma, Principle. Now working as a freelancer, I nurture each day my visual competencies, improving the usability of existing websites or creating modern and user-focused platforms.
